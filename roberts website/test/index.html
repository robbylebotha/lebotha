    Our Humble Beginnings

    Moshodi’s Farm is commercial Bonsmara farm and the aim is to breed Bonsmara for the meat market and to supply young upcoming black farmers with breeding cattle in the future in oder for them to improve their herd. We also breed boerboels which are registered with the South African Breeder’s Society (SABBS) membership number 0664063 and SA Studbook our boerboels are working dogs are important to help guard the cattle and provide security for the family and farm. Moshodi’s farm produces a number of small crop such as African Birds eye chili (Piri Piri) Roma tomatoes in summer , spinach and beetroot. Because of the devastating drought of 2015 we had to be innovative and turn our produce to a product that we could market and sell. I visited an uncle of mine who has traveled the world and shared a recipe that he had learned in Portuguese african speaking nations that has a kick , hot and tasty so i gave it try it tasted nice , i took some to work people tasted , people loved i got my first orders , we named the chill product Firepower. Moshodi’s Farm is run by Bogosi Moshodi Serei, born Soweto South Africa on a cold July morning somewhere in the late 80’s grew up in the Vaal Triangle, Gauteng . Growing up in suburbian South African personally had no farming ambitions , until my mother bought a agricultural land in 2004. Cattle Farming wasn't a compliantly new concept , i learned most of my farming and cattle breeding from my grandparents the Kwape’s.
